What is this?

This is a development page for a Super Metroid Esports Open-Source backbone

How will this help the SM speed running community?

It may help the SM speed running community, when the SM speed running community helps to collaborate! ^_^

What are some of the design goals?

Centralization of all SM speed running links, resources, schedules, daily news, who's-who, community roles, social media, statistical assets, how-to's, onboarding tutorials, community guidelines, and technical assistance (from the community)

Will this be a paid site?

The community must collaborate to show that they have a following that is considered its own esports sub-group. Having together established a sub-grouping, collaboration with paid, and unpaid, supporters would seem appropriate, having followed and discussed legal guidelines pertaining to sponsorship, and the portrayal of copyrighted content.
