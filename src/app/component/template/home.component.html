Statement of Purpose

To lead the way for the active development, engagement, participation, and collaboration of the people of Super Metroid Esports!

What are Super Metroid Esports?

Super Metroid Esports are groups of people that enjoy playing the Super Metroid game, (and other related games), as part of a fun, interactive, online virtual sport; and who often race for non-for-profit organizations as a way to help others.

What does it mean to participate?

The people of Super Metroid Esports meet regularly to discuss topics that are of importance to the community of participants. There are a great number of things to consider if one wants to become a full-time participant. Regular agendas are set by the community board to help people get exposure, meet other participants, get technical assistance; and to help prepare them for a future with the community.

While enjoying showcasing their talents participants also are tasked with: business; legal; human relations; and networking outreach to help inform the community of important news and information on a regular basis.

How do I get in touch with people?

You can reach out to anyone in the community who has left their contact information here. You can also come to a regular monthly meeting for new participants. We look forward to seeing you very soon!
